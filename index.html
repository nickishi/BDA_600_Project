Introduction

Economic uncertainty poses a variety of concerns for early professionals entering the industry. The impact of the pandemic, for one, is one major contributor to the volatility in not only the stock market, but also employment. Layoffs during the beginning of the pandemic were most notable across service-oriented roles, such as leisure and hospitality, while recent layoffs seem to be mostly affecting the tech industry, which was previously immune to the crash most other industries faced (bls.gov). The goal of this project is to understand the relationship between layoffs and company performance, as well as compare the performance across various industries.
